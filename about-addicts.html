About Office Addicts

Office Addicts is a friendly online community for fans of The Office. Born from a shared love of quoting episodes on repeat, it has grown into a welcoming space where members share memes, behind‑the‑scenes tidbits and host live trivia nights.

Run entirely by volunteers, the group doesn’t allow spam or advertising – it’s a place to make friends, laugh together and relive your favourite moments. Whether you’re a casual viewer or a die‑hard, you’ll find your people here.
